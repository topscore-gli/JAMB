🚽 Chapter 8: Excretion

Complete Consolidated Notes – Nephron, Hormones, Structures & Plant Excretion

📌 1. Definition & Significance of Excretion

Definition: Excretion is the removal of metabolic waste products (toxic substances produced during cell metabolism) to prevent harm and maintain internal balance (homeostasis).

🔥 Why It Matters

  • Prevents toxicity from wastes like ammonia, urea, CO₂
  • Maintains water/salt balance (osmoregulation), pH, blood pressure
  • Without excretion → poisoning or fatal imbalance → death
🧠 MEMORY TRICK:

"Excrete or die-crete" — Remove waste or suffer the fate!

👤 3. Excretory Mechanisms in Humans

i. Kidneys (Main Organ – Nephron Functions)

Three major processes:

  • Ultrafiltration (glomerulus + Bowman's capsule): High pressure filters water, urea, salts, glucose → glomerular filtrate (\~180 L/day). Large molecules (cells, proteins) stay in blood.
  • Reabsorption (mainly PCT): Glucose (100%), amino acids, \~70% water/Na⁺/Cl⁻/HCO₃⁻ returned to blood.
  • Secretion (PCT/DCT): H⁺, K⁺, drugs, NH₄⁺ added to tubule.

Loop of Henle: Countercurrent multiplier creates medullary gradient (300–1200 mOsm/L) → enables urine concentration.

Collecting duct: ADH controls final water reabsorption → urine concentration.

ii. Lungs

Remove CO₂ + water vapour during exhalation.

Alveoli in lungs – site of gas exchange (O₂ in, CO₂ out as excretory product)

iii. Skin

Sweat glands excrete water, urea, salts, small amount of ammonia. Also helps thermoregulation.

⚖️ 5. Hormonal Regulation of Kidneys

Main Hormones & Triggers

  • RAAS / Aldosterone: Low BP / low volume / low Na⁺ → ↑ Na⁺ & water retention → ↑ BP
  • ADH (Vasopressin): High osmolarity / low volume → ↑ water reabsorption → concentrated urine
  • ANP: High BP / high volume → ↑ Na⁺ & water excretion → ↓ BP
Hormone Trigger Main Effect Net Result
RAAS / Aldosterone ↓ BP, ↓ volume ↑ Na⁺ & water retention, K⁺ secretion ↑ BP & volume
ADH ↑ osmolarity, ↓ volume ↑ water reabsorption Concentrated urine
ANP ↑ BP, ↑ volume ↑ Na⁺ & water excretion ↓ BP & volume
🧠 MEMORY TRICK:

"RAAS raises, ADH concentrates, ANP lowers – balance restored!"
